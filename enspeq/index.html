Tomographic instruments
for quantum matter.

EnspeQ develops experimental tools that reconstruct effective Hamiltonians, correlations, and ground-state structure of quantum materials and devices — beyond conventional spectroscopy.

Concept
From spectroscopy to tomography

Traditional measurements infer properties indirectly. EnspeQ treats the experiment itself as a physical eigensolver, reconstructing state and interaction structure directly from measurement.

What we build
Hardware + protocol

Cryogenic-compatible measurement modules, control protocols, and analysis pipelines designed to extract maximal information from limited and noisy quantum data.

Applications
Where ambiguity matters

Molecular junctions, superconducting devices, correlated materials, catalytic systems, and quantum hardware where the nature of the ground state is uncertain or device-limiting.

Contact
Early collaborations

EnspeQ is engaging with academic and industrial partners interested in probing systems where standard characterisation has reached its limits.
